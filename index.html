I am Benjamin Ferrer, an antique enthusiast from Lyon, France. My background in French literature has given me a unique perspective on the art and stories behind each piece I collect.

After spending years in Asia, immersing myself in its rich cultures, I returned with a deeper understanding and appreciation for antiques from around the world.

In the end, we conserve only what we love. We will love only what we understand. We will understand only what we are taught. – Baba Dioum.
I strive to share the beauty and history of antiques, teaching others to love and conserve these treasures.

I invite you to explore a collection curated with passion, integrity, and a deep respect for the past. Here, every antique is more than an object; it's a piece of history, waiting to be cherished again.
